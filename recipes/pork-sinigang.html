Pork Sinigang

Description

A comfort food for most Filipinos.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly cut into 4 mm chucnks
  • tamarind soup base
  • 4 pieces of tomatoes
  • 4 pieces Okra
  • salt to taste
  • 1 medium size raddish
  • kangkong
  • lady finger pepper

Procedure

  1. Boil the pork belly until fork-tender.
  2. Add the tomatoes and raddish, boil for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add the tamarind base and salt to taste.
  4. Add the kangkong leaves and the lady finger pepper.
  5. Simmer for 3 more minutes.
  6. Serve piping hot with steamed rice.
