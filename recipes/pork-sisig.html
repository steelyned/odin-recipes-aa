Pork Sisig

Description

A dish that is relished as a viand and is also good with beer.

Ingredients

  • Pork head and ears
  • chicken liver
  • 2 large onions
  • 3 tbsp of calamansi juice
  • 2 tbsp of white vinegar
  • pepper and salt to taste
  1. Boil the pork head and ears until fork-tender.
  2. Broil the pork head and ears until crispy.
  3. Chop the pork head and ears to small bite size.
  4. Broil the chicken liver.
  5. Mash the chicken liver to a paste.
  6. In a bowl, mix the calamansi juice and the other ingredients.
  7. Mix the chicken liver paste, chopped pork head and ears.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve in a hotplate.
  10. Garnish with lady finger peppers.
