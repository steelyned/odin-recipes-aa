Chinese Pork Belly

Description

One of the best Chinese restaurant recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Shaosing wine
  • Rock Sugar
  • Soy sauce and oyster sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Five spice powder
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Green onions for garnish

Procedure

  1. Blanch the pork belly.
  2. Fry the pork belly in hot oil.
  3. Boil in soy sauce, garlic and ginger.
  4. Simmer until the meat is tender.
  5. Add the five spice powder, salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve with hot rice and garnish with green onions.
